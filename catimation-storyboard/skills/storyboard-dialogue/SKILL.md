Dialogue Rules:
8. If screenplay provides character names, USE THEM EXACTLY. Never guess from visual style.
9. If screenplay provides dialogue, EXTRACT VERBATIM. Never fabricate lines.

## Example

Screenplay: `ELENA: "We don't have time."`
- ✓ Caption keeps the name "Elena" and the line verbatim: "We don't have time."
- ✗ Do NOT rename her "the woman" or paraphrase to "She says they're in a hurry."
